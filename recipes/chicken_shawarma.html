Chicken Shawarma

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs
  • 1 large red onion, peeled and quartered
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 2 tbs chopped fresh parsley
Marinade
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled, smashed, and minced
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • A pinch groun cinnamon
  • Red pepper flakes, to taste

STEPS

  1. Mix together marinade ingredients in a medium-sized container with an air tight lid.
  2. Add chicken, and let it marinate in fridge for 12-24 hours.
  3. Preheat oven to 400.
  4. Add onions to the dish.
  5. Cook for 30-45 minutes, using a meat thermometer to determine when chicken is ready.
  6. Toss tbs. of olive oil and fresh parsley to the dish.
