Jackson Salad Dressing

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 3 tbs cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp Coleman mustard
  • 3 tsp spicey brown mustard
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 cup light olive oil
  • Garlic powder to taste
  • Optional: grated parmesan cheese to taste

STEPS

  1. Puree onion and vinegar.
  2. Add Coleman mustard and spicey brown mustard to mix and blend well.
  3. Add olive oil, garlic powder, sugar, salt, pepper, and parmesan cheese.
  4. Blend until creamy.
